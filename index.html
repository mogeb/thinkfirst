About ThinkFirst

We're two blokes with a misinformation chip on our shoulder.

Why are you doing this?

Like many people, we are fed up with misinformation online. The issue is much bigger than the poorly conceived "fake news" label. Sensationalism, selective fact omission, false or missing context, and even satire can all be considered misinformation. With all the noise online, we—perhaps foolishly—decided to take on Goliath and see if we can demonstrate a more thoughtful way to consume information.

How did this start?

As many a great idea in the modern age, this was born over a heated chat based on a Facebook comment. The fateful comment was a simple suggestion: collect examples of the world's worst news in a database for people to reflect and learn from them.
